What's Included

Everything Under One Roof. One Invoice. One Team.

Coordinating multiple vendors for a single exhibition is exhausting — and risky. One delay from a contractor cascades into a missed deadline, a half-built stall, and a show you're not ready for. Turnkey eliminates all of that.

With Approach Media as your single point of contact, every phase is owned, managed, and delivered by one accountable team. You brief us once, approve the design, and walk into your stall on show day — ready to meet customers.

  • ✦
    Design & Concept — Complete 2D/3D stall design aligned to your brand
  • ✦
    In-House Fabrication — Built in our own factory with quality controls
  • ✦
    Logistics & Transport — Safe delivery from our workshop to the venue
  • ✦
    Installation & Setup — Expert crew installs your stall on time, every time
  • ✦
    AV & Tech Integration — Screens, LED walls, interactive displays, all set up and tested
  • ✦
    Dismantling & Storage — Post-show breakdown and warehousing if required
